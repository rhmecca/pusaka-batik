Transparency in digital preservation. This page documents the artificial intelligence technical specifications, model architecture, and the dataset utilized to train the Pusaka Batik identification system.

Architecture & Performance

EfficientNet-B3 (Deep Fine-Tuning)

Production

This system utilizes a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) architecture based on EfficientNet-B3, enhanced through Deep Transfer Learning. The model is specifically optimized to recognize micro-textures and complex geometric patterns inherent in traditional wax-resist dyeing techniques.

Test Accuracy

82.33%

247 of 300 correct predictions

Convergence

149 Epochs

Early Stopping Implemented

Optimizer Adam (Learning Rate: 1e-5)
Loss Function Categorical Crossentropy
Input Resolution 300 x 300 pixels (RGB)

Dataset Specification

DATASETv7 - Batik Nusantara

Source: Kaggle Repository | Augmentation: Active

View Dataset

The model was trained on a comprehensive corpus of high-resolution image data encompassing 15 classes of traditional motifs. The data preprocessing pipeline includes pixel normalization (1./255) and advanced data augmentation techniques (rotation, shear, width/height shifts, and zoom) to significantly enhance the model's robustness and generalization capability.
